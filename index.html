JEE Response Analyser

Here you can upload your JEE Mains 2nd Attempt Responses and get calculated marks along with brief analytics.

How to upload?

  • Login to the NTA Portal.

  • Open the responses page (Click the BTech button to open the responses page in a new tab)

  • After opening it, hit Ctrl+S (or basically save the webpage) anywhere in your device

  • After saving, hit the Upload Responses button below and upload the HTML file generated from previous step.

Note: Only HTML pages are accepted. If you're on mobile you might get an .aspx file instead of .html. In that case, considering logging in through a PC and then repeating above steps.
